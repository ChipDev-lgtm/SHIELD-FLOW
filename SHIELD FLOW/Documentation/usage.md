# Usage

## Overview

- This is the main menu. In your screen you see some quick actions to our main features, and see some important numbers like
  - Total amount of alerts (Check Local Alerts for more info)
  - Account status
  - Security level (Ideally you would want this to be high)
---
## Malware Scanner
- This section will allow you to scan files and folders for malware, before it can harm your computer.
- You can choose a file and run our scan by clicking "Run Local Scan"
- You can also choose to scan the entire folder using ShieldFlow Scan "Upload & Scan (ShieldFlow)"
---
## Local Alerts
- This section will give you more information on
    - Scans (Tells you the file you scan and gives the result on if it is clean or not)
    - Logins into the application
    - Anything we send to your email
---
## Gmail Monitor 
- In this section, you can use your connected Gmail account to check for any security alerts
- Once scanning is done, you can see any alerts, or see that your email is safe.
---
## Settings
- In this section, you can see your username, email, and our security notice.
